Frequently Asked Questions

Can I turn any image into a 3D model?

While our AI is versatile, it performs best with clear subjects, distinct foregrounds, and solid backgrounds. Highly complex scenes, heavily textured subjects, or transparent objects may need abstract or unexpected results.

What images work best?

Illustrations, character sheets, product photography, and objects with a clear silhouette. We recommend using images with good lighting, contrast, and one main subject.

What export formats are supported?

You can currently export your generated model as GLTF, GLB, or OBJ files. These are industry standards ready for use in most 3D software, games, AR, and web viewers.

Are the models textured?

Yes, the AI attempts to map textures and colors from your original 2D image onto the generated 3D geometry for a cohesive look.
